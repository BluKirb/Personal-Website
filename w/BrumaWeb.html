BRUMA

4HGAMES STUDIO

STORY

"Bruma" is a story about reconstructing a memory. A story divided and distributed into pieces through a city. Everything tells. Each action, mechanics and element of the game is a piece of the story, and therefore, of the memory that the player is building. Bruma is a beautiful hand drawn 2D exploration metroidvania that invites you to the journey of Luna, a cat, searching for the memories of her old human friend, an old lady that lost them.

HISTORY/DEVELOPEMENT

While still being in development, Bruma is an indie project being created by a small team of only 5 people. The team is called 4HA Studios and is located in Valparaíso, Chille. With a planed release of January 2026, Bruma originally began as a pitch idea in 2019. Throughout the time of development the crew has faced many challenges as first time game developers. With the primary goal of user enjoyability and experience, 4HA Studios promise to deliver the best quality product they possibly can. (Attached below is where the budget is going.)

LOCATIONS

Bruma takes place in a beautifully illustrated hand-drawn city named Valparaiso. Valparaiso isfilled with diverse districts, each containing fragments of Luna’s missing memories. From the hills to the sea the silhouettes of houses and machines reveal a port city slowly submerged into the mist. The city is apparently abandoned by humans, giving place to different gangs of stray animals that claim control of different parts of the city. Within Valparaiso is 8 interconnected zones, with areas that can be played in present and past. Luna will embark on a journey to discover the memories of her friend, which will reveal piece by piece the hidden story of this city.
